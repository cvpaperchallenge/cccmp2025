Cvpaper Challenge Conference and MIRU Mentorship Program (CCCMMP)

The “Cvpaper Challenge Conference and MIRU Mentorship Program (CCCMMP)" is a Japanese symposium on image recognition and understanding, as well as language, audio, robotics, and machine learning. Whether you belong to a university, a private company, or simply have a keen interest in cutting-edge research within this domain, CCCMMP provides an ideal platform for networking, collaboration, and socializing with fellow researchers and industry professionals.

Authors guideline

  • Paper format
    • Page Limit: Papers must not exceed 8 pages, excluding references and appendices.
    • Templates: Authors are required to use the provided LaTeX templates available on CCCMMP2025 website. The future template link will come here.
  • Submission Process
    • Online Submission: All submissions must be made via the OpenReview system.
    • Anonymous Review: Ensure that the submitted paper is anonymized for double-blind review. Authors' names and affiliations should not be included in the initial submission.
    • File Format: Submissions should be in PDF format.
  • Review Process
    • Submitted papers will undergo a peer-review process.
    • Each paper will be reviewed by at least 2 independent reviewers.
  • Presentation Guidelines
    • Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by one of the authors.
    • Detailed presentation guidelines will be provided upon acceptance.
